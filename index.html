About

What is Songwise?

Songwise is the all-in-one online application that makes it easy and fun to learn to read music through singing songs.


More About Songwise

Story

Why Songwise?

In her work as a music director in the United States and Scotland, Andrea Grody noticed that many actors in musical theater do not know how to read music. She conceived of Songwise as a solution a the problem many musical theater actors face - music training that is accessible, comprehensive, engaging, and flexible.

We are working to bring Songwise not only to those who cannot read music in musical theater but also to all of those who perform without being able to read music, whether it be in choirs, karoake rooms, or in the shower!

Features

How does it work?

  • Songs

    Learn New Songs

    Within minutes of starting each lesson, you’ll be reading and singing an actual song, complete with lyrics and a backing track.

  • Exercises

    Sing Along, Then Sing Alone

    Get going from the get-go with several short exercises in each lesson. Listen first, then sing along, and finally sing on your own.

  • Drills

    Solidify Your Learning

    Hone your skills with short, fun drills that further develop your musical understanding.

  • ... And More

    Develop Special Skills

    As your learning advances, you can gain access to specialty lessons that teach skills like riffing, harmonizing, and accompanying yourself on a piano or guitar.

What We Do

Reading music is an essential skill for musicians, the bridge between “I sing in the shower” and “I am a singer,” But for singers to want to learn to read music, the only available resources are expensive and inconvenient, and the content they offer is scattered and unfocused. There are no resources that know how to take singers from zero to reading music AND that show them how to get there.

Enter Songwise, an all-in-one online platform that makes it easy and fun for singers to learn to read music. From the very first lesson, students are reading and singing real songs. With short and effective lessons available online or on the go, musicianship has never been more accessible.

Songwise proudly serves its mission to give all singers the tools and the confidence to be the musicians they’ve always wanted to be.
